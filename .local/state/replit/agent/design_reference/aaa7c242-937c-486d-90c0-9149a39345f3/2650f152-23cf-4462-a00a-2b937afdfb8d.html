Frequently Asked Questions

Do you upload my files?
No, all processing is local. Your files never leave your browser. We use modern web technologies to process images entirely on your device.
What metadata gets removed?
We remove GPS coordinates, device/camera information, timestamps, XMP/IPTC blocks, embedded previews/thumbnails, and software information. This includes location data that could reveal where photos were taken.
Which formats are supported?
JPEG/JPG, PNG, and WebP formats are fully supported. HEIC and AVIF can be processed if your browser supports local decoding (newer browsers and devices).
Is there a file size or quantity limit?
There are no artificial limits. However, processing is limited by your device's memory and processing power. We recommend processing batches of 10-20 images at a time for optimal performance.
